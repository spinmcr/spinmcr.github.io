We are SPIN

We are a charitable organisation based in Manchester, tackling the ongoing issue of homelessness and poverty. Here you can find support if you're experiencing homelessness or poverty, or you can volunteer and support people in need.

Our mission is to help and support vulnerable and socially-excluded people living in poverty and rough-sleeping in the streets of Manchester

We've got big plans

SPIN keeps growing, but so does the number of people that seek our help and support. We rely entirely on donations. With our entire managing team working for free and all of the money donated going into refurbishing our center and operating around Manchester, we put every penny into good use.

Follow us on Facebook, we post regularly and we keep you up to date with our operations.

Whether you are an academic who is skilled at writing grant applications, an excellent cook, or someone with a drive to help the less fortunate, we could really use your help in order to achieve our goals. Get involved as a volunteer, find out more about us or donate to our cause.
